# Zoho

## Introduction

In this document, we provide a step-by-step guide on how users can integrate Zoho in their Wave account to sync contacts across their Zoho ecosystem.

## Intergrating Zoho in Your Wave Account

1.  [Login](https://app.wavecnct.com/login) to your Wave user account
2.  Go to Settings, then click on Integrations.
3.  Click on the '**Connect**' button.<br /><br />
    <img src="/connect-zoho.png" alt="Connect Zoho" width="500px" />
    <br />
4.  A pop-up will open, allowing you to turn on contact auto sync between your Wave and Zoho accounts. We recommend leaving this on. Press '**Continue**.' A new tab will open asking you to authenticate your Zoho account.<br /><br />
    <img src="/zoho-auto-sync-contacts.png" alt="Toggle Auto Sync" width="500px" />
    <br />
5.  Provide your Zoho credentials in the fields provided. Press '**Log In**.'
    <Callout type="info">
    **Note**: The account you authenticate must be a Zoho administrative account to ensure your integration works properly.
    </Callout><br />
6.  After reviewing the permission prompt, press '**Accept**' to grant Zoho access to your Wave account data. You will now be re-directed to your Wave account.<br /><br />
    <img src="/zoho-allow-access.png" alt="Allow Access" width="500px" />
    <br />

    <img src="/disconnect-zoho.png" alt="Disconnect Button" width="500px" />

    **Note**: Your Wave contacts will automatically sync as '**Contacts**' in your Zoho account.
