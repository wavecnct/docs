# Hubspot

## Introduction

In this document, we provide a step-by-step guide on how users can integrate HubSpot in their Wave account to sync contacts across their HubSpot ecosystem.

## Intergrating HubSpot in Your Wave Account

1.  [Login](https://app.wavecnct.com/login) to your Wave user account
2.  Go to Settings, then click on Integrations.
3.  Click on the '**Connect**' button.<br /><br />
    <img src="/connect-hubspot.png" alt="Connect HubSpot" width="500px" />
    <br />
4.  A pop-up will open, allowing you to turn on contact auto sync between your Wave and HubSpot accounts. We recommend leaving this on. Press '**Continue**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/hubspot-auto-sync-contacts.png" alt="Toggle Auto Sync" width="400px" />
    <br />
5.  A new tab will open asking you to authenticate your HubSpot account. Press '**Sign in to your HubSpot account**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/authenticate-hubspot.png" alt="Authenticate Your HubSpot Account" width="400px" />
    <br />
6.  Provide your HubSpot credentials in the fields provided. Press '**Log in**.'
    <Callout type="info">
    **Note**: The account you authenticate must be a Salesforce administrative account to ensure your integration works properly.
    </Callout>
    <br />
7.  In the following page, select the HubSpot account you wish to integrate to your Wave account. Press '**Choose Account**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/hubspot-choose-account.png" alt="Select Desired HubSpot Account" width="400px" />
    <br /> 
8.  After reviewing the permission prompt, check the agreement box to grant HubSpot access to your Wave account data. Press '**Connect app**.' You will now be re-directed to your Wave account.<br /><br />
    <img src="/hubspot-allow-access.png" alt="Allow Access" width="500px" />
    <br />
