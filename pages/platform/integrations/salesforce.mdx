# Salesforce

# Introduction

In this document, we provide a step-by-step guide on how users can integrate Salesforce in their Wave account to sync contacts and lead across Salesforce ecosystem.

1.  [Login](https://app.wavecnct.com/login) to your Wave user account
2.  Go to Settings, then click on Integrations.
3.  Click on the '**Connect**' button.<br /><br />
    ![Connect Salesforce](/connect-salesforce.png)
4.  A pop-up will open, allowing you to toggle on contact auto sync between your Wave and Salesforce accounts. We recommend leaving this on. Press '**Continue**.' A new tab will open asking you to authenticate your Salesforce account.
    ![Toggle Auto Sync](/auto-sync-contacts.png)
5.   Provide your Salesforce credentials in the fields provided. Press '**Log In**.'
    <Callout type=info>
    **Note**: the account you choose to log-in with must be a Salesforce administrative account to ensure your integration works properly.
    </Callout>
6.  After reviewing the permission prompt, allow Salesforce to access your Wave account data. You will now be re-directed to your Wave account.
    ![Allow Access](/allow-access.png)
