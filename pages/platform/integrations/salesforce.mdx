# Salesforce

## Introduction

In this document, we provide a step-by-step guide on how users can integrate Salesforce in their Wave account to sync contacts and lead across their Salesforce ecosystem.

## Intergrating Salesforce in Your Wave Account

1.  [Login](https://app.wavecnct.com/login) to your Wave user account
2.  Go to Settings, then click on Integrations.
3.  Click on the '**Connect**' button.<br /><br />
    <img src="/connect-salesforce.png" alt="Connect Salesforce" width="500px" />
    <br />
4.  A pop-up will open, allowing you to turn on contact auto sync between your Wave and Salesforce accounts. We recommend leaving this on. Press '**Continue**.' A new tab will open asking you to authenticate your Salesforce account.<br /><br />
    <img src="/salesforce-auto-sync-contacts.png" alt="Toggle Auto Sync" width="500px" />
    <br />
5.  Provide your Salesforce credentials in the fields provided. Press '**Log In**.'
    <Callout type="info">
    **Note**: The account you authenticate must be a Salesforce administrative account to ensure your integration works properly.
    </Callout><br />
6.  After reviewing the permission prompt, grant Salesforce access to your Wave account data. You will now be re-directed to your Wave account.<br /><br />
    <img src="/salesforce-allow-access.png" alt="Allow Access" width="300px" />
    <br />
7.  Verify a successful integration of Salesforce by spotting the â€˜**Disconnect**â€™ button across Salesforce.<br /><br />
    <img src="/disconnect-salesforce.png" alt="Disconnect Button" width="500px" />

    <Callout type="info">
    **Note**: Your Wave contacts will automatically sync as '**Leads**' in your Salesforce account.
    </Callout>

Congratulations ðŸŽ‰, you have successfully integrated Salesforce in your Wave account.

### Contact Owner Assignment:

Wave Connect automatically assigns Lead/Contact owner based on the creator's email. If the email does not match with any Salesforce user, the default value specified in your Salesforce account applies.

## Manually Add Wave Connect External ID:

1.  Navigate to '_Lead_' in'_Object Manager_' under '_Setup_.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/object-manager-lead.png" alt="Natigate to Leads Settings" width="700px" />
    <br />
2.  Verify that '_WaveConnectId__c_' exists under '_Fields & Relationships_.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/wave-connect-external-id.png" alt="Check For Wave Connect External Id Under Fields and RElationships" width="700px" />
    <br />
3.  If '_WaveConnectId__c_' does not exist, create a new field by clicking '**New**' under '-Fields & Relationships-.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/new-fields-relationships.png" alt="Add a New Field to Fields & Relationship" width="700px" />
    <br />
4.  Select 'Text' for '_Choose the field type_' and press '**Next**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/new-field-step-one.png" alt="Setup New Field: Step One" width="700px" />
    <br />
5.  Enter the following details for 'Enter the details' then press '**Next**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/new-field-step-two.png" alt="Setup New Field: Step Two" width="700px" />
    <br />
6.  Confirm '_Standard User_' is checked under '_Established field-level security_' and press '**Next**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/new-field-step-three.png" alt="Setup New Field: Step Three" width="700px" />
    <br />
7.  Under '_Add to page layouts_,' select all layouts you wish to add this field to then press '**Save**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/new-field-step-four.png" alt="Setup New Field: Step Four" width="700px" />
    <br />
8.  If '_Wave Connect External Id_' already exists under '_Fields & Relationships_,' click its name to navigate to its properties then press '**Set Field-Level Security**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/hubspot-field-level-security.png" alt="Navigate to Field-Level Security" width="700px" />
    <br />
9.  Confirm '_Standard User_' is checked under '_Field-level security for profile_.' Press '**Save**.'<br /><br />
    <img src="/standard-user-checked.png" alt="Check if Standard User is Checked" width="700px" />
    <br />
